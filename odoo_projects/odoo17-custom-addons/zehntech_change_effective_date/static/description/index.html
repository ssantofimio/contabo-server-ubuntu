Change Effective Date

The Change Effective Date module allows users to modify the effective dates of key business transactions in Odoo, including invoices, sales orders, purchase orders, and stock operations. It supports both backdating and future-dating, enabling accurate financial reporting, inventory valuation, and currency rate application based on the selected date. Role-based access ensures only authorized users can edit validated documents, maintaining data integrity and control. Seamlessly integrated with Odoo’s Accounting and Inventory modules, this feature streamlines date management while enhancing administrative flexibility and compliance.

FAQs

Q1. Can all users change the effective date in Odoo?

All users can set the effective date before validation. After the document is validated, only admins or users with the "Change Effective Date" privilege can make changes.

Q2. What happens when the effective date is changed?

Changing the effective date updates related valuations, currency rates, and reports based on the selected date, ensuring accurate backdated entries.
